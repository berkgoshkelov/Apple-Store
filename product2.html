The iPhone 11 Pro offers an impressive set of specifications. Additionally, it incorporates True Tone technology to adjust the display's color temperature according to the ambient lighting conditions.

Powering the iPhone 11 Pro is the Apple A13 Bionic chip, which includes a third-generation Neural Engine for enhanced machine learning capabilities. With its 64-bit architecture and the embedded M13 motion coprocessor, the device delivers excellent performance and efficient handling of motion-related tasks.

In terms of memory, the iPhone 11 Pro is equipped with 4GB of RAM, allowing for smooth multitasking and responsive performance across various applications.
